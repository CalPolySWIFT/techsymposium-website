About Tech Symposium

Tech Symposium is a student (university) driven event that enables students and professionals to come together for interactive talks, workshops, and networking. As a student organization, we aim to bring the conference experience to students. However, as a community partner, we aim to connect the cybersecurity industry and community to students.
