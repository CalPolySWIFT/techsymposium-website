About SWIFT Tech Symposium

For over seven years, SWIFT has been running Tech Symposium, a conference dedicated to System Administration, Network Administration, and Security—the very foundations of SWIFT. Tech Symposium is a student-run event that allows students and professionals to collaborate through interactive and informative presentations and workshops. With the dynamic and ever-changing role of technology, providing informative and relevant information is crucial to adapt to a continuously changing environment.

While the talks and workshops change every year, one thing stays the same: Tech Symposium will always be free for students. Tech Symposium is closely integrated to universities and most importantly the students. There has always been a forum for students and professionals to explore mutual interests and learn from one another, an aspect that sets Tech Symposium apart from other conferences.

Why should you present? You will inspire professionals and students with your company's technology or through demonstrations of new skills to those willing to learn. In addition, you will develop relationships as knowledgeable mentors to undergraduate students which is an opportunity most college students do not get. Moreover, interacting with students and other professionals from other fields will give you a chance to expand your network or recruit prospective employees.

As with previous years, we aim to offer various trainings, a varied line-up of talks, and many opportunities to connect.

Everyone is more than welcome to attend!
