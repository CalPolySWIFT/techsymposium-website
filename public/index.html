About Tech Symposium

Tech Symposium (TS) is a student-led conference that celebrates the different elements in cybersecurity as well as fundamental system administration and networking concepts. As a university-driven event, TS enables students, professionals, and community members to come together for interactive talks, workshops, and networking.

We pride ourselves on educating our student population about all things related to the tech industry, such as topics including: "Introduction to Mac Forensics", "Virtualizing With Docker", "Data Visualizations with Tableau", and many more topics that are waiting to be shared. Our next Tech Symposium will be on March 13th, 2021 so please stay tuned for more announcements and updates, and contact us if you would like to get involved in our event.

About SWIFT

Students With an Interest in the Future of Technology (SWIFT) is a cybersecurity and technology organization at the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. With a focus in subjects related to cybersecurity, networking, and system administration, we strive to inspire students to take their learning beyond the classroom through our workshops, demonstrations, and research teams.
